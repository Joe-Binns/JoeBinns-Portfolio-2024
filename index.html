Emotion Detection Model

September 2023 - January 2024

The project explored machine learning for emotion detection through facial recognition. It aimed to advance understanding of how to employ algorithms to interpret human emotions accurately from facial expressions. The project utilized the FER2013 dataset to train a model capable of categorizing emotions

My approach began with a RandomForestClassifier. After hyperparameter optimization with GridSearchCV, And finally Integrating PCA. A deep learning model implemented with Keras significantly improved the accuracy to approximately 81.16%.

Sklearn, Keras, Tensorflow, FER2013 dataset
